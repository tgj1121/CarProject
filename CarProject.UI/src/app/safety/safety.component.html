Safety Features of a Car


Safety Belt

Keeps passengers secured to the car seat and allows them to get the full benefits of the air bags

Lower Anchors and Tethers for Children

Makes child seat installation more secure.

Brake Assist

When driver initiates a panic stop, brake assist makes the stop at the shortest distance by preventing the wheels from locking up.

Forward Collision Warning

Automatically detects for crashes. Alerts the user and can even deploy emergency braking.

Pedestrian Detection

Visual cues, audio cues, and emergency braking to detect pedestrians.

Blind Spot Warning

Receive visual cues if a vehicle is in the blind spot of a car

Backup Camera

Helps prevet crashes by making parking easier as well as being able to see if there are any vehicles or pedestrians behind your car.

Parking Assist

Embedded sensors that detect if you are getting to close to surronding objects.

Lane Assist

Assistance for alerting the driver if they have steered out of their lane and enable lane-keeping assist to gradually steer you back into your lane.
